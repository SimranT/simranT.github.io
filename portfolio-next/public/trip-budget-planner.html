What's inside

Simple on the surface, real BI underneath

Budget-driven activity triage

Rate each activity 1–5 on "worth it." If the trip goes over budget, the sheet tells you which low-scoring, high-cost activities to cut first — not just that you're over.

Deadline radar

Visa due dates, insurance windows, cancellation deadlines, and passport expiry all flag amber then red automatically as they approach.

Trip Health Score

One number combining budget adherence, emergency buffer remaining, and how much of your pre-trip checklist is done — check it the way you'd check a fitness ring.

Multi-currency, done properly

Log costs in whatever currency you actually paid in — the sheet converts everything back to your home currency automatically.

Collapsible sections

Flights, stays, activities, packing, and documents all live on one input sheet — grouped so you can collapse what's already filled in.

Built from real trips

Stress-tested with actual Bali and Turkey trip data, not made-up placeholder numbers.
