What this taught me

A couple of lessons baked into this lab

The loudest request isn't always the highest-impact one

Sales wants the integration, Twitter wants the redesign — but the number that actually moves retention or revenue is the one worth building around.

Sometimes the right call is just the more defensible one

Not every decision has a clean, no-downside answer. Naming the trade-off you're accepting is part of making the recommendation, not a weakness in it.
