XP Challenge

One-page SQL challenge

Find the top spending customer per month. Try it, then reveal the elegant solution.

customers_orders(customer_id, customer_name, order_month, amount)

-- Your task: for each order_month, find the customer
-- with the highest total amount.
WITH monthly AS ( SELECT order_month, customer_name, SUM(amount) AS total_spend, RANK() OVER (PARTITION BY order_month ORDER BY SUM(amount) DESC) AS rnk FROM customers_orders GROUP BY order_month, customer_name ) SELECT order_month, customer_name, total_spend FROM monthly WHERE rnk = 1;

💡 Pro analyst secret: RANK() beats a nested subquery here — it's more readable and handles ties gracefully. This exact pattern shows up in real interviews constantly.
